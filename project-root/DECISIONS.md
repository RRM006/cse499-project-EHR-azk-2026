# DECISIONS — Architecture Decision Records (ADR)

> Every time a real, non-trivial choice gets made (a library, a model, a
> schema shape, an API contract between two modules, a rejected approach),
> log it here. This is what stops you and Claude from relitigating settled
> questions every few sessions, and it's gold for writing your thesis
> methodology section later — you'll have a dated paper trail of *why* you
> chose what you chose, not just what you chose.

**Format:** one entry per decision, newest at top. Keep each entry short —
a paragraph, not a page.

---

## Template

```
## D00X — [short decision title]
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Module(s) affected:** 
**Decision:** 
**Why:** (the reasoning / tradeoff)
**Alternatives considered:** (and why rejected)
**Status:** Active / Superseded by D00Y
```
